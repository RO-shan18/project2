CHAR DHAM YATRA

In Hindi, 'char' means four and 'dham' refers to religious destinations. Among the countless religious sites and circuits that devotees visit in Uttarakhand, one of the most prominent is the Char Dham Yatra.

Kedarnath

Kedarnath Temple is one of the most sacred temples and the holiest Hindu pilgrimages in India.

Badrinath

Badrinath or Badrinarayana Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu. It is situated in the town of Badrinath in Uttarakhand, India.

Yamnotri

The sacred shrine of Yamunotri, source of the river Yamuna, is the westernmost shrine in the Garhwal Himalayas, perched atop a flank of Bandar Poonch Parvat.

Gangotri

the most sacred of all rivers, Ganges (or Ganga), descended from heaven to earth at Gangotri, when Lord Shiva released the mighty river from his locks.
